How It Works

Simple to start, deep to master

1
Enter the Garden
You begin with 100 coins at the garden entrance. The maze sprawls before you.
2
Explore the Paths
Navigate branching hedgerows using arrow keys or on-screen buttons. Discover the garden's secrets.
3
Find a Challenge
5 glowing puzzle rooms are hidden throughout the maze. Each one unlocks a different brain challenge.
4
Solve & Earn
Solve the puzzle to earn 20 coins. Use hints or extra time when you need them.
5
Reach the Exit
Solve all 5 puzzles to unlock the garden gate. Victory grants you infinite coins forever.

About This Project

Built to help,
designed to delight

Garden Maze was created with a simple belief — games should do more than entertain. Every puzzle in this game is chosen because it trains a real cognitive skill that matters in everyday life.

Pattern recognition helps children understand mathematics. Memory training supports long-term learning. Logic puzzles develop reasoning skills that carry into every career. And exploration — wandering the maze, discovering what's around the corner — nurtures natural curiosity.

This project is built with love, and it will always be free to play. Because access to good brain training shouldn't depend on how much money you have.
